What‘s your Impact?

Welcome to the Impact Exhibit, here at the Musuem Name! The goal of the exhibition is to collect data about your eco-habits, and to create a visual representation of our community, and how our different Global Footprints come together to affect the world.

To make the data we receive a little more interesting for you, we've paired a quiz together with a quick game of Minesweeper. Take the quiz, play the game, and get a printed poster (printed on recycled paper, of course), that we'll hang here in the exhibition.

Your Impact

Thank you for participating! Feel free to try again; we will build another Minesweeper game based on your quiz. However, remember that the objective is not to "win" the game, but to get a better understanding of how difficult it can be to be eco-concious in our current world.

If you're satisfied with your result, head on over to the printer. Someone there will have your result printed out as a poster to be hung in the exhibition, as well as a postcard of your creation you can take home with you.
